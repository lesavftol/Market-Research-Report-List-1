<p><p>Unified Endpoint Management (UEM) software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based UEM solutions are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability and easy access without the need for on-premises infrastructure. They enable organizations to manage endpoints from anywhere, facilitating real-time updates and collaboration. Web-based UEM, on the other hand, operates through a web interface, allowing users to manage devices directly from their browsers. This type emphasizes user accessibility and often integrates seamlessly with existing web applications.</p></p>
