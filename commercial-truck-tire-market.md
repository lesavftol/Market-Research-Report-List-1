<p><p>The commercial truck tire market is divided into two primary types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) tires and replacement tires. OEM tires are those specifically designed and manufactured for new trucks by the vehicle manufacturer, ensuring optimal performance and compatibility. In contrast, the replacement tire market caters to existing trucks needing new tires due to wear or damage. Replacement tires often offer various brands and specifications to meet diverse performance, durability, and cost requirements, catering to fleet operators and individual truck owners.</p></p>
