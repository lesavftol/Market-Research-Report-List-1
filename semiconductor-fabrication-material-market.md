<p><p>The semiconductor fabrication material market comprises various essential components. Silicon wafers serve as the foundational substrate for semiconductor devices. Photomasks provide patterned layers crucial for lithography. Photoresists are light-sensitive materials used in etching patterns onto wafers. Wet chemicals facilitate cleaning and etching processes. CMP slurries and pads assist in chemical-mechanical planarization for surface smoothness. Gases are utilized for different deposition and etching processes, while sputter targets serve as material sources during thin film deposition. Photoresist ancillaries support overall lithography processes, with "other" categories encompassing additional specialized materials.</p></p>
