<p><p>The ski and snowboard wax market is divided into two main types: glide wax and grip wax. Glide wax enhances the smoothness and speed of skis and snowboards on snow, reducing friction. It's crucial for downhill performance and is available in various temperature ranges. Grip wax, on the other hand, provides traction for cross-country skiing by adhering to the snow underfoot, enabling skiers to push off effectively without sliding backward. Each type serves a specific purpose to optimize performance in winter sports.</p></p>
