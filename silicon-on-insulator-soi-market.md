<p><p>Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) is a semiconductor fabrication technique that uses a silicon layer separated from a bulk silicon substrate by an insulating layer, typically silicon dioxide. This structure enhances performance by reducing parasitic capacitance, improving electrical isolation, and enabling faster switching speeds, which are critical for advanced electronic devices. SOI technology is widely used in applications such as microprocessors, radio frequency integrated circuits, and low-power embedded systems.</p><p>The Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-performance and energy-efficient devices in consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications sectors. The rise of Internet of Things (IoT) applications and advancements in 5G technology further propel the SOI market, as these trends require efficient semiconductor solutions. </p><p>Furthermore, the expanding application of SOI wafers in RF and analog devices is contributing to market expansion.
