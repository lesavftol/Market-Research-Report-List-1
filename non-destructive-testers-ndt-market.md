<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) involves techniques to evaluate materials and structures without causing damage. Key types include Magnetic Particle Testing, which detects surface and near-surface flaws in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields; Ultrasonic Testing, which uses high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects; and Liquid Penetrant Testing, which reveals surface discontinuities by applying a dye that seeps into cracks. Additionally, the "Others" market encompasses methods like radiographic and eddy current testing, highlighting the diverse approaches in ensuring material integrity.</p></p>
